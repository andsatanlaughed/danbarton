### BIO
*(Paste this exactly)*

My name is Dan Barton. Most people who know me well call me GRAMPS —
partly because I have six grandchildren, and partly because that's just who I've become.

I was a pastor for 35 years across six states.
A Hospital Chaplain for 16. A Hospice Chaplain for 15.
I've sat with people in the hardest moments of their lives —
and I've had more than a few hard moments of my own.

I've been married to Kathy for 47 years.
That's not a footnote. That's a credential.

In 2026 I'm doing something I probably should have done years ago:
putting it all down in writing.
Books. Growth concepts. Stories. Poems. A vlog eventually.
A company — And Satan Laughed, LLC —
that has made exactly zero dollars so far
and is perfectly okay with that for now.

I write for men who feel disqualified.
Men who look at their past and think their God dreams are over.
They aren’t.
I know because God has repurposed the skeletons in MY closet.

Pull up a chair. This is going to get good.
