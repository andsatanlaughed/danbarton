About Dan

71 Years. Still Learning. Still Writing. Still Laughing.

Pull up a chair. This is going to get good.

My name is Dan Barton. Most people who know me well call me GRAMPS — partly because I have six grandchildren, and partly because that's just who I've become.

I was a pastor for 35 years across six states. A Hospital Chaplain for 16. A Hospice Chaplain for 15. I've sat with people in the hardest moments of their lives — and I've had more than a few hard moments of my own.

I've been married for 47 years. That's not a footnote. That's a credential.

In 2026 I'm doing something I probably should have done years ago: putting it all down in writing. Books. Growth concepts. Stories. Poems. A vlog eventually. A company — And Satan Laughed, LLC — that has made exactly zero dollars so far and is perfectly okay with that for now.

I write for men who feel disqualified. Men who look at their past and think their God dreams are over. They aren't. I know because God has repurposed the skeletons in MY closet.

The Mission

. . . And Satan Laughed! exists to inspire Christ followers who feel like they have been marginalized or disqualified, to re-capture their God-given dreams — enabling them to share their HIStory and impact their world.
